Lioness !

Lioness do the hunting and lions are the first to eat food

  • Weight : 126 kg
  • Height: 110 cm
  • Statut: Laziest
  • Habitat: Africa

An adult lioness's roar can be heard as far as 5 km away Lioness sometimes feed on leopards, Lioness don't see humans as prey unless they are extremely hungry, Lioness are the only member of the cat family that have a tasseled tail, These wild creatures are mind blowing but only less than 50,000 of them are left in the world. Lets spread awareness to save them!
